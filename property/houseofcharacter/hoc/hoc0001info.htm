Triq Il-Kbira - B'Kara

A charming House of Character in a central location where attention to detail has been paramount in it's renovation.

Once part of an extensisve Palazzo, this house has been rennovated to give a comfortable atmosphere.

The main entrance consists of traditional Mlatese wooden double doors and gives immediate access into the lounge which has exposed stone walls. The high cieling gives a grand appearnce but also serves to keep the lounge area both airy and cool. The lounge area measures approx. 5.4m x 2.9m.

Moving on from the lounge is the dining area measuring 2.3m x 1.1m. To the left of the dining area is a compact modern fitted kitchen measuring 1.8m x 1.5m

Coming out of the kitchen to the left again is the main bathroom measuring 2.9m x 1.4m and is completely tiled with a bath/shower combination, wash-hand basin and mirrored fitted cabinets, giving full use of space.

Moving out of the bathroom and to the left again one travels up the marble staircase, with wrought iron handrails to the first floor landing. Through glazed double doors one finds the first bedroom measuring 5.6 x 2.9m which benefits from restored traditional tile floors. This bedroom also benefits from a balcony with wrought iron handrails overlooking the street below.

To the right of this bedroom is the master bedroom measuring 3.8m x 2.9m, again benefitting from traditional tiled floor and has also utilised old style "Niche" as built-in storage cupboards.

Returning to the landing one can access the spiral staircase leading to the washroom measuring 3m x 1m. This wasshroom also benefits from a modern shower unit at the far end and additional storage space above the spiral staircase.

Access to the roof is gained through a new aluminium door leading onto the main roof itself. This has been treated with waterproofing membrane and measures approximately 6.7m x 6.1m
