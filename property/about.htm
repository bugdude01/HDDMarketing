About HDD Property

HDD Marketing came into being in January of 2006. Based on the sunny mediterranean island of Malta it was initialy set up as an IT Consultancy with only one client! Since then it has grown and developed other areas of operation including, web-design & web-hosting, Domain registration, import and distribution of childrens toys and more recently, supply of optical equipment.

HDD Property began operations in April of 2008 and is a new way to sell your home in Malta & Gozo ONLINE without needing to pay the large fees normally associated with Real Estate agencies and other methods. It is also the BEST way for potential buyers to have a good look before making an appointment for a viewing.

Having been through the real estate process ourselves a number of times, both as buyers and sellers, we thought there had to be a simpler and more cost-effective way to buy and sell your home. The answer we came up with was HDD Property.

We are NOT knocking other methods. Indeed many of our associates advertise on our site as you can see for the more "traditional" real estate agencies, and we fully recognise the need and place where these organisations exist. However, if all you want to do is simply BUY, SELL or RENT your home, HDD Property makes it MUCH easier for MUCH LESS MONEY!
