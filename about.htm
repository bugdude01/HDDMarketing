About Us

HDD Marketing came into being in January of 2006. Originally based on the sunny mediterranean island of Malta it was initialy set up as an IT Consultancy with only one client! Since then it has grown and developed other areas of operation including, web-design & web-hosting, Domain registration, import and distribution of childrens toys and more recently, supply of optical equipment.

HDD Optical started operations in January of 2008 with one specific aim in mind, to deliver top quality stylish spectacles to the Maltese public, at incredibly LOW prices. Although we do not offer the "hundreds" of frames that other optical stores on the island claim to, we do offer a comprehensive range for all tastes. You will NOT find "designer" frames with us as we are of the opinion that you should be able to correct your vision in a stylish way WITHOUT breaking the bank! We could offer you Gucci, Prada, Chanel, Police and all the others but that would mean that our prices would end up just as high as everyone else, and that is NOT what we are about.

We are not knocking the competition, on the contrary. There are many fine establishments that offer a good service and those "designer" names that some people tend to crave. However, once you have discovered that for the same price as one pair of "designer" FRAMES, you could purchase three or even four COMPLETE pairs of glasses from HDD Optical, you may want to re-think how you spend your hard-earned cash!

We only offer best quality spectacles that are manufactured in the UK to stringent EU standard. All our glasses are backed with a full One-Year warranty against defects and are usually delivered within 5 days of your initial order. Why not contact us today to arrange an appointment or a home visit. All you need is your prescription and an eye for a bargain!
