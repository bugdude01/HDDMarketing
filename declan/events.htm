As many of our streets are not allowed to hold a street party on the day of the Royal Wedding, some mums from St. John's decided to bring the street to the school on that Friday and raise some money in the process. Why not join us on Friday 29th April? Come down if you can, there's fun for all the family and the wedding will be shown on the big (and we DO mean BIG!!) screen too!

Declan's Mum's Leap of Faith

Declan's mum (Marlene) will be doing her first tandem parachute jump on Saturday 7th May. What started out as an idea, soon turned into reality when Declan's sister told pretty much THE WHOLE SCHOOL that she was going to do it. To be fair though, Marlene is really up for the challenge and has even admitted to looking forward to it. The jump is going to take place in Cambrige and we are hoping for good weather. Marlene will be bringing sponsor forms to the school and passing to friends and relatives. If you would like a sponsor form to help persuade some of your colleagues, friends and family, please let us know and we will be happy to send some forms over to you. Or if you prefer you can make a donation via PayPal.
