Welcome to our site!

Declan was born on 16th July 2005 which turned out to be a very traumatic day. After many, many complications, it was announced that he had suffered loss of oxygen and so was given hours to live. Despite the gloomy outlook and being asked to sign a DNR, Declan proved all the top doctors wrong and pulled through but we knew life was going to be hard. At 6 months old, we were given the diagnosis of Athetoid Cerebral Palsy.

The term cerebral palsy covers a number of conditions where the brain doesn't work properly, resulting in problems with movement, posture and coordination. Each person with cerebral palsy is affected in a different way.

(Dr Trisha Macnair last medically reviewed this article in August 2010)

As Declan gets older, his mobility needs become more complex and so we are raising money in order to help with the deposit for a Wheelchair Accessible Vehicle and effective mobility and sensory equipment.
